Can I analyse AFLP data with LFMM ?

Yes. In this case, LFMM tests the correlation between the environmental variable and the presence of the dominant marker. You just need to format to LFMM format with 0 for minor marker and 1 for dominant marker, separated by one or several spaces. There should be no space at the end of a line. One line per individual. 9 or -9 value for missing value.
